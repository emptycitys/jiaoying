2021 Symposium on Information Technology and Education (SITE2021)

Recent Events

  • 2021 3rd Symposium on Information Technology and Education (SITE2021)

    2021 Symposium on Information Technology and Education (SITE2021) aims at addressing a key theme on “Information Technology for Education”, which broadly covers all disciplines of information technology from fundamental research to emerging applications, while emphasizing society-shaping technologies in the modern IT era. This conference will provide a major forum for people to present their frontier research work and discuss the latest breakthroughs.

  • 2021 Workshop on Blockchain and Education (BE2021)

    2021 Workshop on Blockchain and Education (BE2021) will be held in Abu Dhabi, UAE, 04-05 Apr, 2021. BE2021 is the 1st session of SITE2021. It aims at providing a forum for people to present their research work on Blockchain and Education. The submission deadline is 2021-03-30; and the deadline for registration and final paper Submission is 2021-04-01.

  • 2021 Workshop on Body Area Network and Education (BANE2021)

    2021 Workshop on Body Area Network and Education (BANE2021) will be held in Abu Dhabi, UAE, 22-23 May, 2021. BANE2021 is the 2nd session of SITE2021. It aims at providing a forum for people to present their research work on Body Area Network and Education. The submission deadline is 2021-05-13; and the deadline for registration and final paper Submission is 2021-05-17.

  • 2021 Workshop on Internet of Things and Education (IoTE2021)

    2021 Workshop on Internet of Things and Education (IoTE2021) will be held in Abu Dhabi, UAE, 10-11 Oct, 2021. IoTE2021 is the 3rd session of SITE2021. It aims at providing a forum for people to present their research work on Internet of Things and Education. The submission deadline is 2021-10-01; and the deadline for registration and final paper Submission is 2021-10-07.

  • 2021 Workshop on Metaverse and Education (MetaE2021)

    2021 Workshop on Metaverse and Education (MetaE2021) will be held in Abu Dhabi, UAE, 27-28 Dec, 2021. MetaE2021 is the 4th session of SITE2021. It aims at providing a forum for people to present their research work on Metaverse and Education. The submission deadline is 2021-12-20; and the deadline for registration and final paper Submission is 2021-12-25.


Submission

Please submit your abstract paper to Email: site@jiaoying.org. The official language is English. All abstract papers should use this template. The email subject should be named "SITE + Your Name".

If you need translation related services, please contact us via Email: site@jiaoying.org. The email subject should be named "English Service + Your Name".


Registration

Authors need to fill out the registration form received in the acceptance notification. Registration and Abstract Paper Publication Fee: 500USD or 3200CNY.


Publication

All accepted abstract papers will be published by Journal of Computer and Higher Education.
